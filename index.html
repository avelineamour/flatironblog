About Razelle

Razelle is a Boston-based orthopedic massage therapist and student of full stack web development at the amazing ~Flatiron School~. She has a passion for beautiful design, the merging of technology with artistry, solving problems, and coding. You will always find her with a cup of jasmine pearls tea and a soft blanket.
